## TensorBoard

PyTorch integrates natively with TensorBoard via `torch.utils.tensorboard`.

```python
from torch.utils.tensorboard import SummaryWriter

# Initialize writer
writer = SummaryWriter('runs/experiment_1')

# In your training loop:
for epoch in range(epochs):
    # ... train step ...
    writer.add_scalar('Loss/train', loss, epoch)
    writer.add_scalar('Accuracy/train', acc, epoch)

writer.close()
```

<Callout type="tip">
Consider using Weights & Biases (W&B) or MLflow for more robust cloud-based experiment tracking, especially when working in a team.
</Callout>
