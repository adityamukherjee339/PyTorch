A handy reference guide for everyday PyTorch operations.

## Common Operations

<table>
  <thead>
    <tr>
      <th>Operation</th>
      <th>Code Snippet</th>
    </tr>
  </thead>
  <tbody>
    <tr>
      <td>Create tensor of zeros</td>
      <td><code>torch.zeros(size)</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>Create tensor of random uniform</td>
      <td><code>torch.rand(size)</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>Get item from 1-element tensor</td>
      <td><code>x.item()</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>Move tensor to GPU</td>
      <td><code>x.to('cuda')</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>Concatenate tensors</td>
      <td><code>torch.cat((x, y), dim=0)</code></td>
    </tr>
    <tr>
      <td>Matrix multiplication</td>
      <td><code>torch.matmul(A, B)</code> or <code>A @ B</code></td>
    </tr>
  </tbody>
</table>

<Callout type="tip">
Always keep the official PyTorch documentation open. It's comprehensive and includes excellent tutorials.
